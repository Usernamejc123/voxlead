FAQ

Common questions

How long until I start seeing replies?
Most campaigns generate replies within 5-7 business days after approval. We need 2-3 days for prospect research and email warmup, then outreach begins.
Will this hurt my domain reputation?
No. We use separate warmed sending domains and accounts — your primary domain is never touched. All emails are sent through pre-warmed infrastructure with 97%+ deliverability.
What information do you need from me?
Just your business name, what you sell, and your target market (industry, location, company size). We handle everything else — prospect research, email writing, and sending.
Can I review emails before they go out?
Yes. On Growth and Scale plans, we share sample emails for your approval before launching the full campaign. Starter plans use our proven templates optimized for your industry.
What's included in the campaign report?
Every campaign includes a full report: emails sent, open rate, reply rate, bounces, and a list of interested prospects. Growth and Scale plans get weekly progress reports throughout the campaign.
